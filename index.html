About the Artist

Hello! This artist's name is Alexander Rodriguez Bravo, he is the first in his biological family to go to college. Lex knew he wanted to become an artist when he was four years old. He is self taught,
motivated artist who specializes in cartoon characters and anime. Now he enjoys doing digital, graphic art on his laptop and tablet. He also paints and draw freelance.
Lex knows that he can make a good career as an artist in the field that he's pursuing. Lex's career goal is to work for Disney animation studios, Pixar or an animation company to make shows and movies.
